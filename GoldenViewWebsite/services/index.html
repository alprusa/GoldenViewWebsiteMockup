Golden View Senior Services Enables Seniors to Continue Life Independently at Home

Everyone worries that as they age they will be unable to perform simple tasks they once were able to perform. Some may only need a small amount of assistance with tasks while others need far more support. Our caregivers are trained to provide the assisted care that caters to each client's needs.

Personal Care

We provide a wide variety of services to our patients to help them during their day to day activities. We privide available care 1 to 24 hours per day. Our caregivers are trained to perform services you require and are in need of. You are free from tax liabilities as we supply you with your needed caregivers. You are insured against damages and loss. All legal requirements are covered and our staff is licensed and bonded to asssure that you are protected.

Patients get to stay in their own homes while gettinng companionship and general assistance. Caregivers will assist with personal grooming and patient mobility. They will provide bedside care with monitoring and medication assistance. Transportation to desired locations will be provided to the patient. We will keep the patients physician of the patients progress as well.

Benefits

Provided with one on one care. The hospital settings will generally assign many clients to one caregiver leaving you to often have to wait for services. The services you are paying good money for should not be delayed by understaffed facilities.

With in home elderly care services we also allow you to maintain control of you, your home, and your belongings. Everyone likes to be in their familiar and comfortable surroundings. Our own homes are exactly that for each and everyone of us.

With living in facilities there are strict rules on visitation, however, being at home allows for vistors to come at any time of the day. Many people don't have easily flexible schedules so visiting during set times limits how often family and frinds and visit.

We address every individuals needs as we know no one has the same needs. We first consider what needs you as an individual are in need of. Then we adopt our services for your personal needs.

Meal Preparation

Our caregivers will shop for your meals and prepare your meals for you. Meals will be planning in a menu that you get to decide on our care givers will not force you to eat meals you do not like. Your meals will be served to you will assitance in eating if you need help. And you do not need to worry about cleaning up our care givers will take care of the clean up of your meals.

House Keeping

We provide light cleaning of the house. Care givers will change bed linens, wash dishes, vacuum, do the laundry, and any other light cleaning needed around your home.
